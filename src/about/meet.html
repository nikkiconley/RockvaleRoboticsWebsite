Meet the team

On the Rockvale Robotics Team, each member has different assigned jobs to help improve the team and product being built. Some members help with designs and engineering, while others help with the coding and computer tech designs. There are even parts in the group who will eventually help with different parts of the competition. Most of the members have helped improve the robot with ideas or hands-on designing. The robot will need to be driven, so the team let’s everyone drive to decide who should be the driver when it’s time for the competition. Artists of the team helps with the designs of the T-shirts the team will were to recognize who they are competing for. Once the finishing design is done, the team will run a few tests on the robot to check for anything new that could possibly be added for the final product.
